About Us

Who We Are

Redemption Tones is a professional live sound and rental company specializing in touring production, festival sound engineering, and event audio solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we've worked with some of the biggest names in music across Europe.

Our team of expert sound engineers and technicians are passionate about delivering exceptional audio experiences, whether it's an intimate club show or a massive festival main stage.

Our Mission

We believe that great sound is the foundation of any memorable live performance. Our mission is to provide artists, bands, and event organizers with world-class audio equipment and technical expertise that brings their vision to life. We're committed to excellence, reliability, and innovation in everything we do.
